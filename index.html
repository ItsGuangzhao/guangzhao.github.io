About

I am a PhD student in mathematics at the University of Alberta, working under the supervision of Nikita Karpenko.

My research interests lie in motivic cohomology, algebraic cobordism, and motivic homotopy theory. I am particularly interested in Weil restriction, norm functors, and their interactions with cohomology theories and cycle class maps.
