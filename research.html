Research Interests

My work is in algebraic geometry and motivic homotopy theory, with a focus on motivic cohomology, algebraic cobordism, and related cohomological constructions.

I am especially interested in Weil restriction, norm functors, Thom spaces, and cycle class maps, as well as their compatibility properties in motivic settings.

Current Projects

  • Weil restriction and motivic étale cycle class maps
  • Weil restriction, normal bundles, and motivic Thom spaces
  • Questions related to algebraic cobordism and cohomological operations
